The Coach

From fat and depressed
to Fit Strong Club.

I'm Rico Armstrong, a NASM-certified personal trainer of 10 years. I've coached over 7,000 group fitness classes and hundreds of personal training clients.

Before I got into training, I was fat and depressed. Years later, I figured out how to lose the weight. Then came another problem: I plateaued. That's when I discovered strength training — and it did more than break the plateau. It built the kind of resilience I'd need to carry whatever life handed me.

“Strength is resilient.”

My only regret is that it took me years to figure this out. I don't want you to take that long to let your body show you how strong you really are.
